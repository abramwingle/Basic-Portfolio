About me

 

I am currently working as a production manager and quality control manager at Allied Painters Inc. We paint everything from old houses to new houses to industrial buildings and everything inbetween. A typical day for me would consist of driving anywhere from Logan, UT to Payson, UT.

My web development journey began when I was a teenager. My dad was a developer and showed me some cool sites that teach you how to code. I played around with it for a few months but I never took it too seriously.

Growing up, my dad was able to build a lot of cool things using his extensive knowledge. It sparked something in me and coding is something that I have been wanting to learn since I was around the age of 14. I first tried learning java but that was a big mistake for a beginner. It kind of turned me off to the programming world and I forgot about it. But when I learned about all the cool stuff you could do with just HTML and CSS, I was hooked. I began a web developement course at the University of Utah in October 2018.
